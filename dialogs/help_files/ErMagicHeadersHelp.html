Earth-Ref Magic Headers Help Window

The MagicBuilder is a Python tool for adding metadata to MagIC project.
The top row in the dialog window shows the headers of the MagIC tables. The required headers are added and cannot be removed. The bottom row in the dialog window shows a list of optional headers.
(Details on MagIC model different headers are in: "http://earthref.org/MagIC/metadata/)
To add or remove headers from your project model: choose the header name and click the add/remove buttons.

After you choose the headers of the tables press the OK button. You will then be guided through a series of windows to check your data and fill in the blanks.
